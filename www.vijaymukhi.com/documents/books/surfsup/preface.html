SURF’S UP

Preface

Are you a mother waiting to tell her son studying in Boston which medicine he should swallow with how many glasses of hot milk to cure his awful cough and cold?

Are you an engineer, a doctor, a businessman or a lawyer searching for information to improve your project, research, company or case study?

Are you a booklover, artist or musician wanting to buy books, magazines, paints, brushes, cassettes and instruments from across the seven seas?

Are you an enterprising and innovative young man wanting to show off your skills to CEO’s in British offices?

Are you a girlfriend waiting to send her French boyfriend greetings for Valentine’s Day?

Are you a loner looking for friends from around the globe?

Are you a Ricky Martin fan, wanting to have his latest pictures and music filling up your room?

Are you a dog lover finding a solution to poor l’il Tommy’s ticks?

Well, if you are any of the above, then you are who we are addressing.

Our book 'Surf’s Up' is the ultimate guide to the Internet, a technology that has taken the world by a storm and touched all aspects of human life in some way or the other. This book is a journey through the world wide web and its objective is to explain the readers the different facilities offered by the Internet. The book deals in great detail with the most important facets provided by the net; easy and quick communication and surplus wealth of information. Written in a simple and lucid style, the book allows those who have absolutely no knowledge of the Internet to successfully get addicted to it.

 

P.S. We have been working on this book for several months. So incase the screens that you see in our book do not correspond with what is currently on the net, fret not. The Internet is dynamic and ever evolving. Websites and Internet services may have changed their appearances and features.
